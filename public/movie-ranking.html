The Best Movies According to Liv

My top movies of all-time

Dead Poets Society

One of the most beautiful and inspiring movies I´ve ever seen, for me it felt like everyting I always wanted to say and never did.

Boyhood

This movie for me has one of the most realistc representations of what growing up feels like. Something I noticed about the footage was how the colors became more neutral as the years were passing.

Stand By Me

Classic 80´s movie, It felt so personal watching those kids dealing with the real mystery of the film: how to say goodbye to your youth?

Good Will Hunting

This movie got me thinking even more about how much wasted potention we have in this world, I loved seeing Will overcome his traumas and start a new life

Before Sunrise

One of the most interesting romance films made in my opinion, it didn´t need a big plot twist, it´s simple and honest just by showing the couple´s connection through dialogue

Alice in Wonderland

My favourite childhood film of all time! I identify a lot with Alice´s desire for a world of her own.
